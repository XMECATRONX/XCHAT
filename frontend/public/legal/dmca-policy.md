# DMCA Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

Xchat respects intellectual property rights and responds to valid DMCA notices.

## 1. Submitting a Notice
Provide:
- Your contact information
- Identification of the copyrighted work
- Identification of the allegedly infringing material
- A statement of good faith belief
- A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate
- Your signature (physical or electronic)

Send notices to: dmca@xchat.example

## 2. Counter-Notice
If you believe content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice with:
- Your contact information
- Identification of the removed content
- A statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake
- Consent to jurisdiction in your local federal court
- Your signature

We will forward counter-notices to the original complainant as required by law.
