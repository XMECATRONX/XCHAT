# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

## 1. Overview
This policy explains how Xchat collects, uses, and protects personal data.

## 2. Data We Collect
- Account data (email, username, profile details)
- Usage data (device, browser, logs, interactions)
- Payment data (handled by payment providers)

## 3. How We Use Data
- Provide and improve the service
- Secure the platform and prevent abuse
- Communicate updates and support

## 4. Sharing
We share data with service providers for hosting, analytics, and payments. We may share data if required by law.

## 5. Retention
We retain data as long as needed for service operation, legal obligations, and security.

## 6. Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

## 7. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may request access, deletion, or correction of your data.

## 8. International Transfers
Data may be processed in countries outside your own, with appropriate safeguards.

## 9. Contact
Privacy requests: privacy@xchat.example
